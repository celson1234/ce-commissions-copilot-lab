Subject: Standard Annualized Revenue (SAR) Tracking and Quota Accelerators
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Standard Annualized Revenue (SAR) Recognition
Standard Annualized Revenue (SAR) is the primary metric used to track monthly sales quotas. A merchant's SAR is officially counted toward a sales representative's monthly quota only when the Merchant ID (MID) reaches InstallApp status in the system.

2. SAR Calculation Methodology
SAR is calculated automatically using the data provided on the merchant application. The system calculates the SAR value using the following formula:

Discount Revenue: Estimated Annualized Volume multiplied by the agreed-upon Discount Rate.

Authorization Revenue: The Estimated Annualized Volume divided by the Average Ticket (yielding the estimated number of transactions), multiplied by the per-item Auth Fee.

Monthly Fees: The total recurring monthly fees multiplied by 12.
The total SAR equals the sum of the Discount Revenue, Authorization Revenue, and Annualized Monthly Fees.

3. Quota Accelerators
The standard baseline revenue share for sales representatives is 20% of the revenue generated by the account. If a representative exceeds their assigned monthly SAR quota by 200%, they trigger an accelerator. This accelerator increases the revenue share on those accounts from 20% to 30%. The accelerated 30% rate is locked in and remains in effect for the first 12 months of the account's lifespan.
