Subject: MID Credits and Install Status Requirements
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. MID Credit Payouts
Sales representatives are eligible to receive a flat $500 bonus credit for every new, active Merchant ID (MID) brought onto the platform.

2. Activation Triggers (Install Status)
To prevent payouts on dormant accounts, the $500 MID credit is not released when the account is approved or when the contract is signed. The credit is only issued when the MID officially reaches Install status.

3. Definition of Install Status
A MID is only flagged with an Install status once the merchant has successfully batched at least $10.00 worth of live transactions through the gateway. Test transactions or failed authorizations do not count toward this threshold. Once the $10.00 batch clears, the status automatically updates, and the $500 credit is added to the representative's upcoming commission cycle.
